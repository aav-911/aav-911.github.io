# Portfolio Website
This will be my portfolio website that will be used to describe who I am as a person and what I have to offer.

## Webpages Included
There is currently a total of four webpages. All webpages will have a navigation bar at the top to navigate between each page. 

### Homepage
This will be the homepage, which shows what I look like, offers the user an introduction to who I am, and offers two buttons that either take the user to the About Me page or the Connect page. 

### Contact
This will be the "Connect" page where the user is offered different ways of reaching out to me. Whether it's through LinkedIn, Handshake, GitHub, or even with my email address.

### About Me
This will be the "About Me" page where I give more information about myself, where I started, where I am, and what are my future plans. This will give the user a better understanding of who am I both personally and professionally.

### My Projects
This will be the "My Projects", which shows my programming, music, and development projects that I have accomplished over my career. I will also be displaying my current and future projects that I plan on completeing.
